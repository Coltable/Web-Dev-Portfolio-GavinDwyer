About Me

Hello!

Hi, my name is Gavin Dwyer, I'm a wannabe film/music director who has a passion for photography and web development. I currently live in Ireland in Co. Meath with my family as I attend college in TU Dublin Blanchardstown. My hobbies include Scouts, rugby, golf and photography. In my spare time I tend to find myself watching movies/tv shows or playing video games.

I made this website with the intention of helping fellow students/teachers understand and notice the usefulness and capability of social medias and how young people in todays socilty use them in there day to day life. I hope this website showcases how useful social media tools can be and helpful they are when it comes to teaching. If you are interested in checking out what social medias I have covered try clicking on the dropdown menu under apps on the top of your screen.
