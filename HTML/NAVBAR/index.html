Welcome to Social Learning!

Hello and welcome to Social Learning! This website was made with the intention of informing students and teachers on what social medias we deem benifital to teaching. Below you may find some useful tips about this website.

Navagation

If your interested in checking out our website more try using the navagation bar on the top right of your screen, it will help you move around the website and is located on every page so you dont get lost.

Social Media's

If your here then that can only mean one thing... You love social media's just as much as we do! So why not check out all the social media tools we cover by hovering over "Apps" in the navagation bar youll be able to see how the amazing apps we cover there.

Education

We at Social Learning believe that social media is part of a new dawn of learning when it comes to technology and students. If teachers/lecturers learn to use social media in relation to teaching it will open new possibilities for learning.

Contact Us

Now that you have the basics, don't hesitate to contact us via our contact us page with a question or a suggestion and a someone will get back to you.
